Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our services

How do I book a service?

You can book any service by visiting our Appointment page, calling directly at +92 319 3241959, or sending a message on WhatsApp. We'll schedule your initial consultation within 24 hours.

What's included in the service packages?

Each service package includes: initial consultation, personalized meal plan, weekly follow-ups, progress tracking, WhatsApp support, and adjustments as needed. Monthly packages include 4 sessions.

Do you offer refunds?

We don't offer refunds on services already rendered. However, if you're not satisfied after the first session, we can adjust your plan or offer a partial refund. Your satisfaction is our priority.

How long is each consultation?

Initial consultations are 60 minutes. Follow-up sessions are 30 minutes. Online consultations are the same duration via video call.

Do you accept insurance?

Currently, we don't accept insurance directly. However, we can provide receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement.
