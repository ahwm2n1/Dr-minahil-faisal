Common Questions

Appointment FAQs

Find answers to common questions about booking appointments

How do I confirm my appointment?

After submitting the form, you'll receive a WhatsApp message from me within 2-3 hours confirming your appointment. Please ensure your WhatsApp number is correct.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment without any charge. Late cancellations may incur a 50% fee.

Do I need to prepare anything for the consultation?

For the first consultation, please have your medical history, current medications (if any), and a 3-day food diary ready. This helps me create a more personalized plan for you.

How long is each consultation?

Initial consultations are 60 minutes long. Follow-up sessions are 30 minutes. Video consultations are the same duration.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfer, and cash for in-person sessions. Online payments can be made via bank transfer or payment links shared on WhatsApp.
