Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about nutrition counseling

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by visiting our Appointment page, calling directly at +92 326 3651982, or sending a message on WhatsApp. We typically respond within 2-3 hours.

What is the consultation fee?

Initial consultation is PKR 2,500. Follow-up sessions are PKR 1,500. We also offer monthly packages that include weekly check-ins and diet plan adjustments.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer both in-person in Sialkot and online video consultations for clients worldwide. Online sessions are conducted via Zoom or WhatsApp video call.

How long until I see results?

Results vary depending on individual factors, but most clients notice positive changes within 2-4 weeks. Sustainable transformation typically takes 3-6 months of consistent effort.

Do you provide meal plans?

Yes, personalized meal plans are created based on your health goals, dietary preferences, lifestyle, and any medical conditions. Plans include local food options and recipes.
